Pizza Pie Recipe

A completed napolean pizza

To cook a pizza you must first create the dough. This is done by rolling out several ingredients with a rolling pin.

Once the dough is created it's time to break out the sauce. Grab your favourite flavour of ragu and dump that red goodness right on top of the dough.

Throw some cheese on the piza and throw it in the oven to cook.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Mozzarella
  • Ragu
  • Bail

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Use flour yeast and olive oil to make dough
  3. Place dough on baking sheet and spread evenly
  4. Add ragu to the dough
  5. Add cheese and basil as well as any other desired toppings
