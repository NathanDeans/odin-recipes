Egg Sandwich Recipe

This is what your egg sandwich will look like

Eggs are a great way to get some protei into your diet.

If you are looking for a quick meal in the morning an egg sandwich is a fantastic choice

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water on the stove
  2. Place several eggs into the pot of water
  3. Once boiling turn the element off
  4. Leave eggs in pot with water for ten minutes
  5. Remove eggs and peel off the shells
  6. Add eggs mayo and salt and pepper into a bowl
  7. Crush and mix ingredients until it becomes a paste
  8. Spread past onto bread
  9. Cut the bread diagonally
  10. Eat
